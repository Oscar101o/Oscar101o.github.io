Salvadoran recipe

Savory sauce

Ingredients

  • Tomato
  • Chili
  • Onion
  • Annatto
  • Water
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Fill the blender with cold water
  2. Add the tomatoes
  3. Add chili, onion, and annatto
  4. Blend for a minimum of 1 min
  5. Take out a pot and place the sauce inside
  6. Add salt and olive oil
  7. Boil for a minimum of 2 min
  8. Enjoy
