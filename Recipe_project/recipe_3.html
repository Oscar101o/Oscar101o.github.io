Crazy Curtido

Ingredients

  • Repollo
  • Carot
  • Chili
  • Apple Vinigur
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Oregano
  • Ginger

Steps

  1. Scratch up the repollo
  2. Scratch up the Carots
  3. Cut the chili
  4. Place items 1 , 2, and 3 in a glass container
  5. Pour water, ginger, and salt
  6. Steer
  7. Put in fridge
  8. Enjoy
