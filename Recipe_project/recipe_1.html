Salvadoran recipe

Classy pupusas

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Salt
  • Dough
  • Cheese
  • Beans

Steps

  1. Pour water in a bowl
  2. Sprinkle salt on water
  3. Mix dough with water
  4. Create dough balls
  5. Make a small hole inside the ball
  6. place some beans and cheese inside the hole
  7. Pat down the ball as if your making a tortia
  8. Place on fire, 2 min on both sides
  9. Enjoy
